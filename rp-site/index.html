What is Apartment Management Software?

Apartment or Property Management Software replaces the need to drop off a paper check at a landlord's or utility's office. This industry already relies on 260,000 businesses and over 780,000 jobs. Why Rent Portal? We are blockchain based, open-source, and will have the lowest cost for landlords or utility companies. Our token allows the price for landlords and tenants to be lower and lower over time.
